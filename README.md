# 🌱 CarbonWise

> **Track. Understand. Reduce.**
>
> An AI-powered sustainability assistant that helps individuals calculate, understand, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized insights and actionable recommendations.

---

## 🌍 About the Project

CarbonWise is designed to make sustainability simple and accessible. Users can input their transportation habits, electricity usage, flight frequency, and dietary preferences to receive a detailed carbon footprint analysis along with AI-powered recommendations for reducing emissions.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Carbon Footprint Calculator

Calculate emissions based on:

* 🚗 Transportation
* ⚡ Electricity Consumption
* ✈️ Air Travel
* 🥗 Dietary Choices

### 🤖 AI-Powered Analysis

Using Gemini AI, CarbonWise provides:

* Personalized sustainability insights
* Carbon reduction strategies
* Environmental impact assessment
* Monthly sustainability goals

### 🏆 CarbonWise Score System

Receive:

* 🎯 CarbonWise Score (0-100)
* 📈 Sustainability Grade (A-E)
* 🥇 Eco Badge

### 💡 Smart Recommendations

Get practical suggestions such as:

* 🚶 Walk or cycle for short trips
* 🚌 Use public transportation
* 💡 Switch to LED lighting
* 🌱 Adopt a lower-carbon diet
* ✈️ Reduce unnecessary flights
